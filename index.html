CLEAN

A Christian Arts Festival. Accra.
20th, 27th + 28th December 2024.

What is CLEAN?

I'm sure you're familiar with what Ghana has become synonymous with in December. This festival seeks to offer something unique amid the existing celebrations.

Designed to bring Christians together, CLEAN offers an alternative way to explore the relationship between Christianity, culture, and the arts.

Events

We are hosting a series of events for Christian creatives to connect, collaborate, and showcase their work:

  • Christ and Canvases - 20th December (interactive painting session)
  • Brunch - 27th December (networking over food)
  • EXPO - 28th December (exhibition, panel sessions, and networking)

Why CLEAN?

Why CLEAN? Because we need to talk about...

  • The relationship between Christianity and Art in the Ghanaian context
  • The relationship between Christianity and tradition/African culture (navigating syncretism, pluralism, and history)
  • How we bring Christian Creatives together
